01 / The Problem

What's Costing You Time

NovaTech Solutions is currently losing productivity to manual processes that don't scale. Based on our discovery conversations:

Each new customer requires 4-5 hours of manual setup across 6 different tools, consuming 80-100 hours per month for 20 customer onboardings with a ~15% error rate.

This is costing your team approximately 80-100 hours per week in manual work, with additional risk from human error.

02 / The Solution

What We're Building

Fully automated end-to-end customer onboarding that triggers on Stripe payment and provisions accounts, docs, channels, and welcome emails across all systems - eliminating manual work and errors while scaling to 10x volume.

Using n8n + Make as the automation backbone, integrated with Stripe, HubSpot, Slack, Notion, Google Workspace, Custom App / eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
A new customer completes payment on Stripe, firing a webhook that kicks off the entire onboarding automation.
Step 02
Process
The automation creates a contact and deal in HubSpot, provisions a dedicated Slack channel with a welcome message, generates personalized onboarding documents from Notion templates, sets up a Google Workspace folder structure, and enrolls the customer in a 3-email welcome sequence - all with built-in error handling and retry logic.
Step 03
Output
Fully provisioned customer accounts across all 6 platforms, personalized onboarding documentation in Notion, organized Google Workspace folders, and a triggered welcome email sequence.
Step 04
Notify
An admin monitoring dashboard provides real-time visibility into onboarding status, errors, and retries for stakeholders.
