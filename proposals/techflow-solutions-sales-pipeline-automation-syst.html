01 — The Problem

What's Costing You Time

TechFlow Solutions is currently losing productivity to manual processes that don't scale. Based on our discovery conversations:

TechFlow Solutions is losing 15+ hours per week to manual CRM data entry, spreadsheet-based lead scoring, and Friday pipeline reports — resulting in 30% of qualified leads falling through the cracks due to missed follow-ups and deal handoffs that lose critical context over email.

This is costing your team approximately 19 hours per week in manual work, with additional risk from human error.

02 — The Solution

What We're Building

Automate the full sales pipeline from lead scoring through deal-close handoff, eliminating manual data work and ensuring no qualified lead is forgotten while giving leadership real-time pipeline visibility.

Using n8n as the automation backbone, integrated with HubSpot CRM, Slack, Google Sheets, Gmail, Stripe, Notion — eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
New lead activity or engagement signals in HubSpot, deal stage changes, Stripe payment events, or a weekly scheduled trigger every Friday morning
Step 02
Process
Automatically score leads based on HubSpot engagement signals, sync deal data bidirectionally between HubSpot and Google Sheets, queue per-rep follow-up reminders, compile pipeline data into a formatted weekly report, and on deal close trigger a multi-step onboarding sequence
Step 03
Output
Updated lead scores in HubSpot, a synced Google Sheets deal tracker, a formatted weekly pipeline report, a Notion onboarding document for new customers, and an automated CS intro email
Step 04
Notify
Per-rep follow-up reminders sent via Slack direct message; weekly pipeline report auto-posted to the #sales-leadership Slack channel; CS rep notified via Slack upon deal-close assignment
