01 / The Problem

What's Costing You Time

Zenith Solutions is currently losing productivity to manual processes that don't scale. Based on our discovery conversations:

The finance team manually processes 300+ vendor invoices per month, with each invoice taking approximately 15 minutes. Five AP staff spend 60% of their time on this repetitive task, resulting in a 4% error rate.

This is costing your team approximately 75 hours per week in manual work, with additional risk from human error.

02 / The Solution

What We're Building

Fully automated invoice processing pipeline that monitors email inboxes, extracts invoice data with AI, matches against purchase orders in NetSuite, routes approvals intelligently, and surfaces exceptions on a real-time dashboard - dramatically reducing manual effort and errors.

Using n8n as the automation backbone, integrated with NetSuite, Email (inbox for invoices) / eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
New vendor invoice arrives in a designated email inbox
Step 02
Process
AI extracts key invoice fields (vendor, amount, line items, dates), matches extracted data against existing purchase orders in NetSuite, routes the invoice for approval based on predefined rules, and flags exceptions or mismatches for manual review
Step 03
Output
Matched and approved invoices are automatically recorded in NetSuite; unmatched or flagged invoices appear on a real-time exception dashboard
Step 04
Notify
Approvers receive automated notifications for pending approvals; the AP team is alerted via dashboard and email when exceptions require manual intervention
