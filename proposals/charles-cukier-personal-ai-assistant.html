02 • The Solution

What we’re building

Random thoughts, reminders, research requests, PDFs to process, HubSpot jobs - everything lands in one chat. The brain figures out what you want, picks the right tool, and replies. No bot teams. No routing mazes. No picking which app to open.

Built on Claude + n8n, integrated with WhatsApp, HubSpot, Google Calendar, Gmail, Fireflies, Google Drive. Designed to remove manual touchpoints while keeping you in full control.

System Flow

01
Triggers
Three ways the assistant kicks in. (1) Inbound: you text or voice-note the WhatsApp number any time, in English or French. (2) Scheduled: cron jobs fire on their own - daily 7 AM digest, Monday chase digest, reminders firing at their set time, hourly Gmail and HubSpot polls. (3) Async events: research memos finishing, a chased reply landing, the brain pings you when something is ready.
02
Process
The brain reads or transcribes your message, figures out intent (capture, reminder, research, HubSpot job, pre-meeting brief), pulls memory of relevant people and properties, and picks the right tool from its toolbox.
03
Output
The answer comes back in WhatsApp, in whichever language you texted in. Reminders fire on time with the why attached. Memos arrive when ready. Briefs return in seconds. HubSpot jobs are prepared and shown to you before they run.
04
Confirm
Anything that sends a message, creates a record, or touches HubSpot waits for your explicit yes. No outbound action without confirmation, ever.

03 • The Skills

What it actually does

Nine skills, one brain. The assistant knows when each one fires - you don't pick which to use, you just text or talk.

01
Capture
Voice or text → auto-filed by person, property, idea, or follow-up. Searchable on demand.
02
Pre-meeting brief
Text 'walking to Evan' and get back 5 to 15 bullets in seconds - open follow-ups, recent activity, last meeting.
03
Reminders
Timed reminders that include the why, not just the what. 'Call Peter - papers (waiting 9 days).'
04
Waiting-on tracker
Live list of every chase, Monday digest of overdue items, auto-drafted follow-ups, tap-to-confirm closure.
05
Daily morning digest
7 AM brief: today's meetings with one-line context, overdue chases, research ready, yesterday's captures.
06
Research memos
Topic in, structured memo out within 1 to 6 hours. Summary, key facts, opportunities, risks, sources.
07
Document parsing
Contract → key-terms summary. Rent roll → proforma in a Google Sheet. Broker PDF → clean contacts ready for HubSpot.
08
HubSpot chat-ops
Paste contacts and it prepares the import, tags, and sequence queue. You reply yes and it runs.
09
Bilingual EN/FR
Text or voice-note in English, French, or franglais. Replies come back in the language you used.
