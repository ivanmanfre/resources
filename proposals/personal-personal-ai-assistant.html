02 / The Solution

What We're Building

One WhatsApp number, one AI, one brain. You text or voice-note anything (random thoughts, reminders, research requests, PDFs to process, HubSpot jobs) and it sorts itself. No bot teams, no routing mazes, no picking which tool to open.

Using Claude + n8n as the automation backbone, integrated with WhatsApp, HubSpot, Google Calendar, Fireflies, Google Drive / eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
You send a text or voice note to one WhatsApp number, any time, from anywhere.
Step 02
Process
The AI brain reads it, understands intent (capture, reminder, research, HubSpot job, pre-meeting brief, etc.), pulls memory of relevant people and properties, and picks the right tool from its toolbox.
Step 03
Output
The action gets executed: contact filed under the right person, reminder set with context, research memo queued, HubSpot contacts imported and tagged, pre-meeting brief delivered in 5 seconds.
Step 04
Notify
You get a confirmation back in WhatsApp. Anything that sends, spends, or modifies external systems waits for your explicit yes before firing.
