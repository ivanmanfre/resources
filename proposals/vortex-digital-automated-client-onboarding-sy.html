01 / The Problem

What's Costing You Time

Vortex Digital is currently losing productivity to manual processes that don't scale. Based on our discovery conversations:

Client onboarding takes 3-5 days per client with 15+ manual steps spread across multiple platforms, consuming roughly 12 hours of manual work per client and bottlenecking account managers.

This is costing your team approximately 12 hours per week in manual work, with additional risk from human error.

02 / The Solution

What We're Building

A fully automated onboarding workflow that eliminates manual steps across all platforms, reducing onboarding time from days to minutes and freeing account managers to focus on client strategy and relationships.

Using n8n as the automation backbone, integrated with Slack, Asana, Google Drive, QuickBooks, CRM / eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
New client record created or marked as 'ready to onboard' in the CRM
Step 02
Process
The automation creates an Asana project from a predefined template with tasks and deadlines, generates a structured Google Drive folder hierarchy for the client, drafts a QuickBooks invoice with client details, enrolls the client into a 3-email welcome sequence, and posts a checklist to the designated Slack channel for the assigned account manager.
Step 03
Output
A fully provisioned client workspace across Asana, Google Drive, and QuickBooks, a triggered welcome email sequence, and a completion tracking dashboard
Step 04
Notify
Account managers receive a Slack checklist notification with onboarding status, and a tracking dashboard provides real-time visibility into onboarding completion across all clients
