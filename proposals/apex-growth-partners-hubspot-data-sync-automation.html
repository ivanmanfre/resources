01 / The Problem

What's Costing You Time

Apex Growth Partners is currently losing productivity to manual processes that don't scale. Based on our discovery conversations:

Three team members spend a combined ~7 hours per day manually syncing data between HubSpot CRM, Google Sheets, and Slack, with each sync taking ~45 minutes and occurring 3x daily across a 25-client portfolio.

This is costing your team approximately 35 hours per week in manual work, with additional risk from human error.

02 / The Solution

What We're Building

Fully automated real-time bi-directional data sync between HubSpot and Google Sheets, automatic Slack deal-stage notifications, auto-generated weekly client performance reports, and a live dashboard for monitoring sync health - eliminating virtually all manual data entry and reporting.

Using n8n as the automation backbone, integrated with HubSpot CRM, Google Sheets, Slack / eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
Changes in HubSpot deal/contact records or Google Sheets data updates trigger the bi-directional sync; deal stage changes in HubSpot trigger Slack notifications; a weekly scheduled trigger generates and emails client performance reports.
Step 02
Process
The automation detects changes in HubSpot or Google Sheets and syncs data bi-directionally in real time, maps deal stage transitions to formatted Slack messages routed to the appropriate channels, aggregates client performance data weekly into structured reports, and logs all sync activity, errors, and statuses to a monitoring dashboard.
Step 03
Output
Synchronized and up-to-date records across HubSpot and Google Sheets, formatted Slack notifications for deal stage changes, auto-generated weekly client performance report emails, and a live sync status and error monitoring dashboard.
Step 04
Notify
Slack channel notifications are sent automatically when deals move stages, weekly performance reports are emailed directly to relevant stakeholders, and the monitoring dashboard provides real-time visibility into sync status and any errors.
