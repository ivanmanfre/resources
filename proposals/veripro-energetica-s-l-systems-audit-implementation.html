01 / The Problem

What's Costing You Time

VeriPro Energetica S.L. is currently losing productivity to manual processes that don't scale. Based on our discovery conversations:

VeriPro's operational stack was built rapidly over the past year and has reached a critical inflection point. The main CLIENTES table contains 82 fields mixing client data, installation tracking, digital signatures, commissions, and automation logic in a single table. External sales teams share editor-level access to the entire base. Multiple Make scenarios run in production with no documentation, no error alerting, and an unknown total count. With new teams onboarding and a target of 100+ daily installations, these structural risks compound into operational liability.

This is costing your team approximately 15-20 hours per week in manual work, with additional risk from human error.

02 / The Solution

What We're Building

A clean, well-structured operational infrastructure where data is properly separated, permissions are role-based, automations are documented and monitored, and the system can scale to 100+ installations per day with multiple external sales teams operating safely and independently.

Using Make (current), with potential migration to n8n based on audit findings as the automation backbone, integrated with Airtable, Make, Fillout, WhatsApp (Meta Business API), Ringover, Gmail, VeriPro App / eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
Systems audit maps every table, field, automation, and data flow across the entire stack. Produces a written findings report with risk classification and prioritized action plan.
Step 02
Process
Airtable restructure decomposes the 82-field monolith into properly linked tables. Permission architecture is built first (before new teams onboard). Parallel build ensures zero downtime during migration.
Step 03
Output
Automation restructure rebuilds all Make scenarios to match the new table structure. Error alerting, failure notifications, and a centralized automation registry are added across all flows.
Step 04
Notify
AI integration layer is scoped and built on top of the clean, structured data foundation. This phase is intentionally deferred until the architecture is stable and reliable.
