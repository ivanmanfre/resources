01 / Overview

What This System Does

This is an end-to-end content operation built on AI agents and workflow automation. It covers the entire lifecycle, from raw topic discovery to published content, lead capture, audience building, and newsletter generation. Everything is orchestrated, and you stay in control at key review checkpoints.

Discover & Research
AI scans call transcripts, competitor posts, and trending research to surface topics your audience cares about. Generates scored suggestions with ICP fit and classifies them as post-ready or lead-magnet-ready.
Plan & Prioritize
A Content Manager Agent builds scored weekly plans aligned with your brand positioning, content pillars, and target audience. It tracks layer distribution, detects duplicates against the last 60 posts, and assigns formats and visual styles.
Generate & Review
Posts, carousels, and lead magnets flow through multi-step AI pipelines with built-in quality gates. An Editorial Agent scores every piece across 6 dimensions. You review at key checkpoints before content moves forward. AI generates branded images (6 styles) and carousel slides (8 styles).
Publish & Capture
Automated scheduling with smart date assignment. Comment-triggered DM delivery via LeadShark. ICP-filtered lead qualification with Apollo enrichment for hyper-targeted email list building. Lead magnets deploy to Webflow with promo posts and DM templates.
Learn & Multiply
Performance tracking identifies winners. The Repurposing Agent escalates top content across formats: text to carousel to lead magnet. A Newsletter Agent curates weekly emails from top performers. The system feeds insights back into planning, getting smarter every cycle.

Everything lives in ClickUp as the command center, with n8n orchestrating the automation and Claude (Anthropic) as the primary AI brain.

04 / The AI Agents

11 Specialized Agents

This system is not one big workflow. It is a team of specialized AI agents, each with a defined role, its own set of reference documents, and clear decision authority. They coordinate through ClickUp and hand off work to each other automatically.

Content Creation
Editorial Agent Quality gatekeeper. Scores topics across 6 dimensions.
Topic Research Agent Mines transcripts, competitors, and trends for topics.
Post Generation Agent Writes full posts with briefs, hooks, and QA gates.
Visual Generation Agent Creates branded images and carousel slides via Gemini.
Content Manager Agent
Chief Strategist. Plans the entire week's content.
RUNS: EVERY SUNDAY EVENING
  • Reads Content Playbook, Brand Positioning, and full pipeline state
  • Analyzes last 60 published posts for duplicate detection
  • Scores every pending topic on 6 dimensions (max 60)
  • Builds 5-7 post weekly plan with formats, layers, and dates
  • Tracks content layer distribution across weeks
  • Assigns carousel styles, image styles, and lead magnet pairings
Lead Generation
Lead Magnet Agent Builds complete downloadable resources across 6 formats.
ICP Filter Agent Enriches leads via Apollo and scores with qualification gates.
LeadShark Agent
Lead Capture and First-Touch Outreach.
RUNS: ON ENGAGEMENT SIGNALS
  • Monitors comments for trigger keywords (e.g., "GUIDE", "CHECKLIST")
  • Sends automated first DMs with resource link + conversation starter
  • Delivers two DM variations: Direct Delivery and Conversation Starter
  • Tracks DM delivery and response rates
  • Routes engaged leads into the qualification pipeline
Intelligence & Growth
Performance Tracker Scrapes LinkedIn metrics daily, identifies patterns.
Competitor Intelligence Tracks competitor activity, extracts patterns and gaps.
Repurposing Agent Turns winners into new formats: text to carousel to lead magnet.
Newsletter Agent
Email Curator. Builds weekly newsletters automatically.
RUNS: WEEKLY
  • Analyzes the week's published posts ranked by engagement
  • Selects top 3-5 performers as newsletter features
  • Generates newsletter copy: intro, summaries, key takeaways, CTA
  • Publishes directly to ConvertKit with formatting and segmentation
  • Grows email list from content engagement

11 / Differentiators

What Makes This Different

01
Brand Depth, Not Templates
The AI writes from a 4,000-word voice document, a positioning strategy, a content playbook, and a forbidden language list. The output sounds like one specific person, not "AI content."
02
11 AI Agents, Not One Prompt
Each agent has a defined role, its own reference documents, and clear decision authority. They hand off work to each other like a real content team. You review at key checkpoints and stay in control.
03
Quality Gates at Every Step
Editorial assessment before generation. QA check after generation. Feasibility gate before lead magnets. Human review before publishing. Nothing goes live without passing multiple layers.
04
RAG-Powered Context
When generating content, the AI pulls from a vector database of past transcripts, competitor posts, and published content. It writes with context, not in a vacuum. The system gets smarter over time.
05
Lead Capture Is Native
LeadShark automates first-touch DMs on trigger words. The ICP Filter enriches every lead via Apollo. High-value leads get routed to direct outreach. Result: a hyper-filtered email list of people who already engaged with your content.
06
Content Never Dies
The Repurposing Agent watches performance and flags winners for format escalation. Text post to carousel to lead magnet to case study. Every winning idea gets progressively deeper treatment. Newsletters curate your best work automatically.
