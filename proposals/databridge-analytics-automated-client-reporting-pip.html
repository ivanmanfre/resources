01 / The Problem

What's Costing You Time

DataBridge Analytics is currently losing productivity to manual processes that don't scale. Based on our discovery conversations:

The team manually compiles data reports from four different sources for 12 enterprise clients, consuming approximately 60 hours per month in repetitive, error-prone reporting work.

This is costing your team approximately 15 hours per week in manual work, with additional risk from human error.

02 / The Solution

What We're Building

A fully automated reporting pipeline that pulls data from all sources, generates branded PDF reports, and delivers them to clients via personalized email - reducing monthly reporting effort from 60 hours to under 5 hours of review time.

Using n8n as the automation backbone, integrated with Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Analytics, PostgreSQL / eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
Scheduled cron trigger runs the pipeline on a defined reporting cadence (e.g. weekly or monthly) to initiate data extraction from all four sources
Step 02
Process
The automation connects to Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Analytics, and the internal PostgreSQL database, extracts the relevant data, transforms and aggregates it into unified datasets, populates branded dashboard templates, and generates polished PDF reports with personalized cover notes for each of the 12 enterprise clients
Step 03
Output
Branded PDF reports per client containing unified, aggregated data from all four sources, ready for delivery
Step 04
Notify
Each client receives their personalized PDF report via automated email with a customized cover note, and the DataBridge team receives a summary notification confirming successful delivery or flagging any errors for review
