01 / The Problem

What's Costing You Time

Meridian Consulting is currently losing productivity to manual processes that don't scale. Based on our discovery conversations:

The 6-person sales team manually qualifies inbound leads from multiple channels, with each rep spending ~2 hours/day sifting through form submissions, LinkedIn messages, and email inquiries. An estimated 40% of inbound leads are unqualified, resulting in 60+ wasted hours per week across the team.

This is costing your team approximately 10 hours per week in manual work, with additional risk from human error.

02 / The Solution

What We're Building

An automated system that ingests leads from all inbound channels, scores them against ICP criteria, routes qualified leads to the right rep, triggers personalized follow-up sequences, and logs everything in HubSpot - eliminating manual qualification work and letting reps focus on closing.

Using n8n as the automation backbone, integrated with Typeform, LinkedIn, Email, HubSpot CRM / eliminating manual touchpoints while giving you full control.

System Flow

Step 01
Trigger
New lead submission via Typeform, incoming LinkedIn message, or inbound email inquiry
Step 02
Process
The automation ingests leads from all three channels, normalizes the data, scores each lead against Meridian's ICP criteria (industry, deal size, fit signals), and routes qualified leads to the appropriate sales rep based on industry and deal size. Unqualified leads are filtered out and tagged accordingly. Personalized follow-up email sequences are triggered for qualified leads.
Step 03
Output
Scored and categorized lead records in HubSpot CRM with automatic rep assignment, follow-up sequences initiated, and unqualified leads filtered and tagged
Step 04
Notify
Assigned sales reps are notified when a qualified lead is routed to them, with lead score and key details included
